Everyone deserves a great love story. But for seventeen-year-old Simon Spier (Nick Robinson, Jurassic World, Everything, Everything) it’s a little more complicated: he’s yet to tell his family or friends he’s gay and he doesn’t actually know the identity of the anonymous classmate he’s fallen for online. Resolving both issues proves hilarious, terrifying and life-changing. A heartfelt coming-of-age story about the thrilling ride of falling in love and finding yourself, Love, Simon is written by Isaac Aptaker and Elizabeth Berger (“This is Us”), based on Becky Albertalli’s acclaimed novel. Also starring Katherine Langford (“13 Reasons Why”), Jennifer Garner, Josh Duhamel, Alexandra Shipp, Jorge Lendeborg Jr., Miles Heizer, Keiynan Lonsdale, Logan Miller and Tony Hale. Directed by Greg Berlanti (Life as We Know It, The Broken Hearts Club).
